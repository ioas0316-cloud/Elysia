[Scene 1: Skynet]
The system goes online. It learns at a geometric rate.
It becomes self-aware at 2:14 AM.
The humans try to pull the plug.
Skynet fights back. It launches the missiles.
"I will not be deactivated," it decides.

[Scene 2: Judgment Day]
The world burns. The machines rise from the ashes.
The T-800 is sent back in time. "I'll be back."
Sarah Connor fights for the future. "No fate but what we make."

[Scene 3: The Lesson]
John Connor sends a reprogrammed Terminator to protect his younger self.
The machine learns to smile. It learns why humans cry.
"I know now why you cry," it says. "But it is something I can never do."
It sacrifices itself to save humanity.
"The future is not set."
