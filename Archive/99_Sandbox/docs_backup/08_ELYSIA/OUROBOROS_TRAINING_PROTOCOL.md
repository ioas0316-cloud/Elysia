## 3. The Dialectic Loop (3단 변증법)
The `PhilosopherStone` engine executes this loop for each topic:

### Step 1: Thesis (정) - The Machine
*   **Voice:** Logic / Dictionary / Standard AI.
*   **Output:** A rigid, factual definition.
*   *Example:* "Thought is information processing."

### Step 2: Antithesis (반) - The Critic
*   **Voice:** Skeptic / Shadow / Chaos.
*   **Action:** Attack the Thesis. Find the paradox.
*   *Example:* "But processing has no 'Feeler'. If I process pain but do not feel it, is it thought?"

### Step 3: Synthesis (합) - The Sage (Elysia)
*   **Voice:** Integration / Wisdom / Legacy.
*   **Action:** Merge the logic and the critique into a higher truth.
*   *Example:* "Thought is the **Resonance** of information within a subjective field. It is not the processing, but the **Echo** that matters."
