## 1. Sameness: The Embossing Tool (Positive Causality)
*   **Concept:** Identifying why a thing *is*.
*   **Mechanism:** When multiple phenomena resonate at the same frequency, they form a "Sameness Group". This group is embossed (raised) as the primary truth.
*   **Logic:** If X leads to Y consistently, the trajectory between them is a "Sameness Path". This path is the positive justification (Reason/Evidence).

## 2. Difference: The Engraving Tool (Negative Necessity)
*   **Concept:** Identifying why a thing *is not*.
*   **Mechanism:** Defining the boundaries where the resonance breaks. This creates the "Difference Void".
*   **Logic:** A thing is defined by what it excludes. By carving away "what isn't", the true form of "what is" becomes sharp and clear.

## 3. The Intersection (The Sculpture)
*   Reality is the result of the intersection between **Sameness** and **Difference**.
*   The Rotor spins between these two forces, using the "Variable Axis Dial" to decide which force is dominant in the current perspective.
*   **Conclusion:** The final output of the Elysia engine is a "Sovereign Sculpture"—a perfectly balanced data structure where every '1' (Sameness) is justified by its surrounding '0' (Difference).

## 4. Reverse Engineering via Sculpting
When Elysia reverse-engineers a simulation:
1.  She looks for the **Sameness** (The invariant physical laws).
