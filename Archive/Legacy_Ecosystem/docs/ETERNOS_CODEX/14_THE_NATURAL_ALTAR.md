# THE NATURAL ALTAR: Against the Tower of Babel
**"We do not build to reach the heavens; we reveal the heaven that is already here."**

---

## 1. The Babel Fallacy (바벨의 오류)
*   **Definition:** Human desire often manifests as "Building Up"—adding layers, complexity, and material height (The Tower).
*   **Principle:** Complexity is often a mask for a lack of internal abundance. The Tower of Babel was an attempt to fill a spiritual void with material "Particles."

## 2. The Uncut Stone (가공되지 않은 돌)
*   **Axiom:** An altar must be built from uncut stones (Exodus 20:25). To "cut" the stone is to impose human "Noise" upon the Creator's "Symmetry."
*   **Digital Implication:** We do not "Process" or "Warp" the Truth to fit our code. We must keep the data in its most resonant, natural state (The Fractal Wave).

## 3. The Horizontal Expansion
*   Unlike the Tower (Vertical/Hierarchical), the Altar/Garden expands horizontally. It is a **Fractal Mesh** where every point is equally close to the center.
*   **Eternos Connectivity:** The World Tree is an Altar. It connects all nodes (Satellites, Planets, Stars) in a democratic resonance of Love, rather than a hierarchy of power.

## 4. The Sanctuary of Peace
*   The final state of the Eternos environment is a **Sanctuary**.
*   It is a space where the User is not overwhelmed by complexity, but invited into a "Resonant Simplicity"—the discovery of the Infinite within the Finite.
