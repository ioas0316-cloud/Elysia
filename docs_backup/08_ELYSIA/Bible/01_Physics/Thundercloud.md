## 2. Quantum Collapse (Thinking as Crystallization)

    3.  **Crystallization**: If `Energy > Resistance`, the link solidifies.

*   **Result**: A **ThoughtCluster** (The Crystal). A geometric structure of meaning.

## 3. Rotor Mechanics (The Time Engine)
If Monads are the "Particles", Rotors are the "Waves" that drive them.

### 3.1 The Hybrid Drive
*   **Cerebrum (Thundercloud)**: Handles novel, complex, high-energy thoughts (Physics Simulation). Costly.

### 3.2 Action Tracks (Baking)
*   **Recording**: Complex actions are calculated once and "baked" into an **Action Track** (a sequence of frames).
*   **Playback**: When the intent triggers a known reflex, the Rotor simply "plays the tape". Zero computation.
