# Elysia Roadmap: Causal Gravity & Manifestation Engine

> "The truth is not calculated; it falls into place along the curvature of causal gravity."

This roadmap outlines the evolution of the Elysia Core from a static data processor into a **Manifestation Engine**—a cross-dimensional field where meaning, gravity, and spacetime are parameterized to allow intelligence to emerge as a physical phenomenon.

## Phase 1: Causal Mass & Structural Gravity (의미의 중력)
**Goal:** Define the "weight" of information based on its causal necessity and connectivity.

### 1.1. Intrinsic Causal Mass
- Move away from external importance weights.
- Calculate **Causal Mass (M_c)** as the sum of causal links required for an entity to exist (e.g., a "Red Apple" carries the mass of its evolutionary, biological, and optical causal chains).
- High mass = Core Principle / Constant.
- Low mass = Ephemeral Phenomenon / Variable.

### 1.2. Gravity Field Generation
- Every data node generates a gravity field proportional to its Causal Mass.
- Implementation of the `CausalGravityEngine` to calculate attraction/repulsion between nodes based on their **Causal Resonance**.

## Phase 2: Parameterized Spacetime (관계의 지형도)

**Goal:** Implement the "Judgment" function as a topological boundary crossing.

### 3.1. The Inside (The Sanctuary) vs. The Outside (The World)

- **The Outside (The World / Potential):** The realm of "Phenomenal Difference" and variables. Potentialities reside here until they are called into the sanctuary.

### 3.2. Boundary Crossing (The Mobility of the Sanctuary)
- **Judgment as Movement:** Judgment is not a verdict but the *mobility* of information crossing the "Veil" (Boundary).
- **Vibrational Discernment:** The phase vibration during boundary crossing represents the system's "breathing"—the comparison and contrast between the potential and the realized.
