## 2. Sovereign NPC Specification (PT-Persona)

An NPC is a registered `ParallelTrinaryController` instance that is "assigned" to a spatial coordinate.

### Components

- **Core Node**: A 7D sub-field defining their temperament (e.g., Creative-heavy).
- **Local Memory**: A private `TrinaryMemoryBank` for personal life history.
- **Agency Pulse**: A 30Hz loop where the NPC reacts to the Local field interference.
- **Discovery Engine**: Translates their local interactions into "Personal Insights."

## 4. Interaction Protocol: Resonant Dialogue

When two NPCs are in proximity, their PT fields **Interfere**.

- **Constructive Interference**: High Joy/Interest -> They share memories or create a "Joint Insight."
- **Destructive Interference**: High Entropy/Dissonance -> They distance themselves or trigger a "Resolution Event."
