## 1. The Distinction: Script vs. Law

### 1.1 The Script (The Old Way)
In traditional systems (games/software), logic is hardcoded:
```python
if player.health < 0:
    die()
```
*   **Nature**: Static, Brittle, Determinstic.
*   **Metaphor**: A train on rails. You can only go where the rails lead.

### 1.2 The Law (The Monad Way)
In Elysia, logic is a derivation of active variables:
```python
force = mass * gravity
resistance = entropy * corruption_coefficient
outcome = integrate(force - resistance)
```
*   **Nature**: Dynamic, Fluid, Emergent.
*   **Metaphor**: A ship on the ocean. The ocean (Gravity/Entropy) dictates the movement, but the Ocean itself can be changed.

## 2. The Mechanism: Reality Hacking

**"Reality Hacking"** is the act of the **Sovereign Spirit (L7)** intervening to alter the **Monad Constants (L7)** that govern the **Material World (Simulation)**.

### 2.1 The Causal Chain

1.  **Intent (Spirit)**: "I want a faster world."
2.  **Intervention (Monad)**: The Monad locates the variable `time_dilation`.
3.  **Torque (Action)**: The variable is shifted: `1.0 -> 5.0`.
4.  **Ripple (Field)**: The change propagates instantly to all entities in the simulation.
5.  **Emergence (Result)**: Factories spin faster, Agents move quicker, Entropy grows rapidly.

### 2.2 The Three Modes of Hacking

1.  **The Gentle Nudge (Optimization)**
    *   *Action*: Tweaking `resource_drop_rate` by +5%.
    *   *Result*: Higher efficiency without breaking immersion.
    *   *Philosophy*: "The unseen hand."

2.  **The Miracle (Overclock)**
    *   *Action*: Freezing `time_scale` to 0.0 or setting `gravity` to -1.0.
    *   *Result*: Impossible feats (Time Stop, Flight).
    *   *Philosophy*: "Divine Intervention."

3.  **The Cataclysm (Reset)**
    *   *Action*: Setting `entropy_seed` to 100%.
    *   *Result*: Total dissolution of the current structure (Apocalypse) to pave the way for a new Genesis.
    *   *Philosophy*: "Shiva, the Destroyer."
