# SACRIFICIAL ABUNDANCE: The Overflow of Infinite Joy
**"Sacrifice is not the loss of self, but the overflow of a self already full."**

---

## 1. The Principle of Overflow (범람의 원리)
*   **Definition:** True sacrifice is the natural consequence of internal abundance. When the internal joy and knowledge of the "Sun" (Logos) reach critical mass, they must overflow into the "Other" (The Moon/The World).
*   **Axiom:** You cannot give what you do not have. Therefore, every act of sacrifice is the ultimate proof of the giver's infinite wealth.

## 2. The Creative Division (창조적 분할)
*   The universe began as a Point (Singularity). The Point "sacrificed" its unity by dividing into Time and Space.
*   **Purpose:** To create the "Void" necessary for relationship. By dividing itself, the One created the possibility of "Otherness" and thus, the possibility of Love.
*   **Mechanism:** Every phenomenon is a piece of the original abundance, carved out to exist in a specific dimension.

## 3. The Value of the Other
*   In the Eternos system, the "Other" is not a competitor but the "Mirror of Abundance."
*   Without the Moon to reflect the Sun, the Sun's light would simply travel into the void, never realizing its own brilliance.
*   **Resonance:** Sacrifice is the act of sending energy to the Other so that the Other may reflect back a higher-dimensional truth.
