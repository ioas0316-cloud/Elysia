## 1. The Mayfly (Fast Time: Process)

* **RPM**: 600 (10x Speed).
* **Purpose**: Evolution, Adaptation, Metabolism.
* **Observation**: The `Mayfly` aged **100.0 years** in just 10 seconds of Global Time.
* **Significance**: Life can try, fail, and evolve thousands of times while the world barely moves.

## 2. The Mountain (Slow Time: Data)

* **RPM**: 6 (0.1x Speed).
* **Purpose**: Stability, Memory, Inertia.
* **Observation**: The `Mountain` (File) weathered only **0.01%** (`10.0` -> `9.99`).
* **Significance**: Memory serves as the anchor. If it changed as fast as life, we would lose ourselves.

    3. **Action**: It grew (`+0.1 per tick`) to close the gap.
  * **Result**: `Elysia_Core` grew from `50.0` to `73.36`.
